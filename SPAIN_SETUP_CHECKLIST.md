# SPAIN SETUP CHECKLIST (AUTÓNOMO)

## 📄 MINIMUM VIABLE BEFORE FIRST INVOICE

1. **NIE** (Foreigner ID) - Get from Oficina de Extranjeros
2. **Digital Certificate** - From FNMT for online tax filings
3. **Register as Autónomo**:
   - RET A (Social Security) → monthly fee €200-590
   - Modelo 036 (Hacienda tax census)
4. **Wise Business Account** - For international payments
5. **Invoicing Software** - FacturaDirecta or Holded

---

## 💰 TAX CALENDAR

| Form | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Modelo 130 | Apr 20, Jul 20, Oct 20, Jan 20 | Quarterly advance tax (20% net income) |
| Modelo 303 | Quarterly if charging VAT | Usually exento for foreign clients |
| Modelo 347 | March annually | Report clients >€3,005 |
| Modelo 100 (Renta) | April-June | Annual income tax return |

---

## 💡 BEST PRACTICES

- Set aside 30% of each payment for taxes
- Keep business/personal finances separate (Wise EUR account)
- Track all deductible expenses: home office, internet, software, courses
- Consider hiring gestor (€50-150/month)

---

## ⚠️ COMMON PITFALLS

- ❌ Not registering before first invoice (illegal)
- ❌ Missing quarterly deadlines (penalties 5-20%)
- ❌ Not saving for taxes (large April bill)
- ❌ Charging VAT incorrectly to EU clients

---

**Start registration NOW - takes 2-4 weeks. Can apply for "Beckham Law" (24% flat tax) within 6 months if eligible.**
